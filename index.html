My second enhancement focuses on advanced algorithmic problem-solving and data structure optimization, applied to the same OpenGL rendering artifact. This milestone reflects mastery of recursive algorithms, transformation stack logic, and trade-off management, as emphasized in my Enhancement Two journal and narrative.
